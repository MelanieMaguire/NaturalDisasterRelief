Welcome to NDR!

This site will help you with all Natural Disaster related things.

Natural Disaster Relief is a web application that provides services such as providing advice for people prepare for a natural disaster, providing insight with news updates, and allowing users to ask/provide help with tasks or finding a missing person during or after a natural disaster.

Services Provided:

Weather Alerts

Stay up to date with the weather in your area. Use NOAA on our site to find any concering weather alerts and stay safe!


Lost/found someone?

Our missing in action database allows you to search for missing people. Found any missing people? Add them, so their family memebers can find them.

Need Shelter?

Look for a shelter near you!
Note: Some shelters may be at capacity. It is always a good idea to call before heading to the shelter to ensure availability.

Need help with anything?

Provide or request help in your community. Need help installing shutters? Ask away! Know how to install shutters? Lend your neighbors a helping hand!


Stay educated on the best ways to prepare for a disaster and avoid preventable damages from occuring.
